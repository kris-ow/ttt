Jo suggests that version 15 is likely needed before meaningful unsupervised scaling occurs, though he cautions against betting on any specific version number.

2. **FSD Approved in the Netherlands — First EU Country:** A Friday afternoon bump in Tesla's stock was attributed to news that FSD received regulatory approval in the Netherlands, marking the first EU country to approve it. 3. **Unsupervised FSD Scaling Remains the Key Catalyst:** Jo emphasizes that the critical unknown is when unsupervised FSD will scale commercially. He expects the upcoming earnings call to be vague on this topic, with Tesla management likely saying they are making "tremendous progress" and that scaling is a function of continuous safety improvements rather than committing to a date. He believes until unsupervised scaling is confirmed, leaps-based options strategies carry meaningful time-decay risk.
