This confirms long-rumored plans to wind down Tesla's flagship legacy sedans/SUVs, representing a significant strategic shift in Tesla's product lineup.

## Key Posts / Themes

1. **Model S/X Production Ending — Official Communication to Owners**
Tesla sent a farewell email to Model S/X owners acknowledging the vehicles' historical role in funding Tesla's technological development, and signaling the end of their production run. The email frames the discontinuation as making way for Tesla's autonomous future (likely Cybercab/robotaxi focus).
