## Humanoid Bots

Robots eliminate the need for oxygen, food, water, radiation shielding, sleep cycles, and exercise, allowing continuous 24/7 operation in environments that would kill humans in minutes. Based on Tesla's target manufacturing costs and projected operational lifespan, the host cites an effective labor cost of approximately $2 per hour for Optimus units.

2. **Optimus production scale and deployment context:** The host states that Optimus is expected to be manufactured at a scale of millions of units per year, which is what makes the economics of using humanoid robots in space viable.
